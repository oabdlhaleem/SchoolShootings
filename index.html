Generation Lockdown: A Map of Mass Shootings in America

Understanding Mass Shootings

Over the last generation we've seen mass shootings become a thing. Within the span of most of our memories they have gone from an unspeakable tragedy to a well discussed (but still tragic) feature of our society.

Over the last decade and a half the terminology of mass shootings has been forced to developed greater sophistication. There are slightly different criteria for an attack to be labled a mass shooting. One common view is that any attack that results in three or more casualties constitutes a mass shooting. The term casualties, though commonly used to mean those killed, refers to both wounded and deceased victims.

Following these standards this map depicts all mass shootings in the United States from 1982 to early 2019.
